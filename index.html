UGC & AICTE Accredited

Bhagwan Mahavir University proudly holds dual accreditations from the UGC and AICTE, symbolizing its unwavering commitment to exceptional academic standards and cutting-edge technical education. These accreditations position the university as a beacon of excellence, offering a high-quality, comprehensive education for students to excel in academia and the professional world.
